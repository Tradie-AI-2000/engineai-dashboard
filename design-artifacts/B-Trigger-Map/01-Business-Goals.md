# Business Goals: EngineAI Dashboard

## Vision Statement
To create an autonomous "Central Intelligence" dashboard that serves as the operational nervous system for Engine AI—orchestrating a digital assembly line of specialist agents to deliver high-margin AI transformations—while providing the architectural blueprint for a rapidly deployable "Business in a Box" for SME clients.

## Strategic Objectives (SMART)

### 1. Scalability Multiplier
*   **Objective:** Achieve founder leverage by automating administrative tasks.
*   **Metric:** Reduction in founder administrative "coal face" time.
*   **Target:** 80% reduction within the first month of deployment.
*   **Rationale:** Success here allows the business to scale without increasing the founders' workload.

### 2. Velocity Benchmark
*   **Objective:** Rapid end-to-end client delivery.
*   **Metric:** Time from "Confirmed Discovery Input" to "Full-Stack Production Build."
*   **Target:** Under 24 hours for 100% of the first 5 test cases.
*   **Rationale:** Speed is a key competitive advantage for the "Business in a Box" model.

### 3. Consistency Score
*   **Objective:** Zero-touch high-quality output.
*   **Metric:** Rate of human refactoring required for agent-generated PRDs and SOWs.
*   **Target:** 0% human refactoring required across all initial client deliveries.
*   **Rationale:** High-quality autonomous outputs build trust and eliminate bottlenecks.
