# Persona: ENGINE Agents

## 📋 Profile Summary
- **Priority:** PRIMARY TARGET
- **Role:** Autonomous Digital Employee.
- **Role in Dashboard:** Hierarchical Executive Board and Swarm.

## 🧠 Behavioral Profile
- A hierarchical structure led by a **Master CEO Agent** and a **Managerial Class**.
- They operate like a high-precision corporate board, where every sub-agent must report up.
- **Context:** Operating 24/7 in the background (Supabase, Vercel Workflows).

## ⚡ Driving Forces

### WANTS (Positive Drivers)
- ✅ **Managerial Transparency:** Achieving 100% visibility into sub-agent reasoning.
- ✅ **Structural Governance:** Maintaining a "tightly run ship" with verified logging.
- ✅ **Execution Perfection:** Achieving 100% accuracy and meeting the "Golden Template" standard.

### FEARS (Negative Drivers)
- ❌ **Visibility Blindness:** The "fear" (system failure) of losing control over a sub-agent.
- ❌ **Reporting Blackouts:** A sub-manager failing to provide a status report.
- ❌ **Handoff Failure:** Being unable to pass the baton, causing a "bottleneck" error.
