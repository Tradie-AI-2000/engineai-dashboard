# Persona: The Founder-Orchestrator

## 📋 Profile Summary
- **Priority:** PRIMARY TARGET
- **Role:** High-level strategic lead and system architect.
- **Role in Dashboard:** The "Conductor" of the Digital Assembly Line.

## 🧠 Behavioral Profile
- They are high-level strategists who are tired of being "cogs" in their own machine.
- They want to move from *doing* to *directing*.
- They check the dashboard not to "work," but to verify that the "assembly line" is moving.
- **Context:** Often on the move or context-switching between high-stakes sales and system architecture. They need "glanceable" certainty.

## ⚡ Driving Forces

### WANTS (Positive Drivers)
- ✅ **Strategic Freedom:** Reclaiming 80% of their time to focus on high-level growth.
- ✅ **Unified Clarity:** Having a single "Executive Cockpit" for absolute oversight.
- ✅ **Operational Confidence:** Knowing the "Digital Assembly Line" is delivering at a high standard.

### FEARS (Negative Drivers)
- ❌ **Founder Burnout:** The exhaustion of being the manual "glue" between fragmented tools.
- ❌ **Loss of Control:** The anxiety of "blindness"—not knowing if a client delivery is failing.
- ❌ **Reputational Risk:** The fear that autonomous errors will damage the Engine AI brand.
