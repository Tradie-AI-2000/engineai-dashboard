# Persona: SME Client

## 📋 Profile Summary
- **Priority:** SECONDARY TARGET
- **Role:** Non-technical business owner.
- **Role in Dashboard:** Recipient of the "Business in a Box" template.

## 🧠 Behavioral Profile
- Overwhelmed by AI hype but desperate for the efficiency it promises.
- They want the *result* (MRR and free time) without understanding the "pipes."
- **Context:** Looking at their dashboard to see if their business is "working."

## ⚡ Driving Forces

### WANTS (Positive Drivers)
- ✅ **Instant Professionalism:** Upgrading their business to "Enterprise Grade" overnight.
- ✅ **Operational Peace:** The relief of having a "Business in a Box" that handles complexity.
- ✅ **Flywheel Momentum:** Seeing business results scale with minimal manual effort.

### FEARS (Negative Drivers)
- ❌ **Tech Alienation:** Fear that they are buying a "black box" they can't control.
- ❌ **Financial Waste:** Anxiety that the investment won't pay off or will require more time.
- ❌ **Operational Fragility:** Fear that if the "AI" breaks, they won't know how to run their business.
