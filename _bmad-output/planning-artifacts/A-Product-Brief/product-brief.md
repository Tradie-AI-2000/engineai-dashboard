# Product Brief: EngineAI Dashboard

## Strategic Summary

The EngineAI Dashboard is the autonomous operational nervous system for Engine AI, designed to transform the business from founder-led operations to founder-led orchestration. By unifying a fragmented tool stack (Linear, Notion, Telegram, Drive) into a single "Executive Cockpit," the dashboard allows Ben and Joe to oversee a "Digital Assembly Line" of specialized AI agents that execute end-to-end client delivery with minimal human intervention.

Initially built for internal efficiency, the dashboard serves as the production-grade blueprint for a rapidly deployable "Business in a Box" template for SME clients. The core value lies in "Decoupled Intelligence"—where the UI provides 30,000ft oversight while specialized BMAD Builder (BMB) modules provide the autonomous logic to drive the 6-stage delivery pipeline (Discovery to MRR) in under 24 hours.

## Vision

To create an autonomous "Central Intelligence" dashboard that serves as the operational nervous system for Engine AI—orchestrating a digital assembly line of specialist agents to deliver high-margin AI transformations—while providing the architectural blueprint for a rapidly deployable "Business in a Box" for SME clients.

**Key Insights from Discussion:**
- **The Delivery Engine:** The dashboard isn't just a UI; it is the engine that executes the 6-stage pipeline (Discovery to MRR) autonomously.
- **Internal vs. External Agents:** EngineAI uses a specialized "BIAB Swarm" for delivery; client clones receive industry-relevant agents.
- **Battle-Hardened Template:** Building for internal use first ensures the white-label model is production-ready.

## Positioning

**Positioning Statement:**
For Engine AI founders who need unified control over an autonomous "Digital Assembly Line," the EngineAI Dashboard is an Agentic OS that orchestrates specialized swarms to drive client delivery from Discovery to MRR. Unlike manual SaaS coordination or static templates, our system initiates action autonomously with production-grade outputs and built-in white-label replication.

**Components:**
- **Target Customer:** Engine AI Founders (Ben and Joe).
- **Their Need:** Unified, autonomous coordination of specialized AI agents across a fragmented tool stack.
- **Product Category:** Autonomous Central Intelligence Engine / Agentic OS.
- **Key Benefit:** Total operational control and elimination of manual handoff "blindness."
- **Alternatives:** Manual SaaS management, standard Notion "Agency OS" templates.
- **Differentiator:** Action-oriented orchestration; the system initiates steps (SOWs, builds) rather than just tracking them.

## Target Users

**Primary Human: The Founder-Orchestrator (Ben & Joe)**
- **Role:** High-level strategic lead and system architect.
- **Goal:** Practice "AI-First" operations by delegating all administrative tasks to agents.
- **Need:** A unified "Central Intelligence" view to assign tasks, oversee agent activity, and monitor business health (MRR, Token burn).

**Primary Agent: The Autonomous Employee (BIAB Swarm, OpenClaw)**
- **Role:** Task-specific specialist (Analysis, PRD, SOW, Onboarding).
- **Goal:** Execute end-to-end business processes with 92%+ accuracy and minimal human input.
- **Need:** Context-awareness via `llms.txt`, clear rules via `agent.md`, and deterministic triggers from the dashboard/Supabase.

## Business Model

- **Model:** Internal Operational Engine (Efficiency-driven).
- **Rationale:** Founders need a "battle-hardened" system first. Focusing on internal efficiency prevents premature complexity before white-labeling.
- **Implications:** Priority is system connectivity (Supabase, Linear, OpenClaw) and autonomous pipeline execution (Discovery -> SOW).

## Success Criteria

**1. The "Scalability Multiplier" (Founders' Leverage)**
- Achieve a state where client volume scales without increasing administrative "coal face" time.
- High-leverage human intervention is only required for high-stakes decision-making.

**2. The "One-Day Delivery" Benchmark (Velocity)**
- Moving from "Confirmed Discovery Input" to a "Full-Stack Production Build" within 24 hours.
- Output is "beautifully packaged" and easy for non-technical clients to implement.

**3. The "Consistency & Confidence" Score (Quality)**
- 100% Client Satisfaction driven by the "Golden Template."
- Agent-generated documents (PRDs, SOWs) require zero human refactoring.

## Competitive Landscape

- **Alternatives:** Manual fragmented stack (Linear/Slack/Notion), "Do-Nothing" burnout ceiling, off-the-shelf passive tracking templates.
- **Unfair Advantage:** Pragmatic, grassroots problem-solving (Founders' background) combined with "Practice what you Preach" high-integrity infrastructure.
- **Why You Win:** The dashboard acts as the **Conductor** of existing tools, not just an island of data.

## Constraints & Context

- **Technical:** Must integrate Telegram, Discord, Google Drive, Gmail/Calendar, Claude ecosystem, and existing Discovery web app.
- **Stack:** Next.js 16, Vercel AI SDK v6, Supabase, Vercel Workflows.
- **Timeline:** 1 WEEK for working MVP.
- **Brand:** Strict adherence to @BRAND.md (Dark, premium, gold-accented, "Operator" aesthetic).
