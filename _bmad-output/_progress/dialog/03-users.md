## The Conversation

## User Profiles

### Primary Human: The Founder-Orchestrator
- **Role:** High-level strategic lead and system architect.
- **Goal:** Practice "AI-First" operations by delegating all administrative tasks to agents.
- **Frustrations:** Potential "bureaucratic drag" and fragmented tool context (Notion, Linear, Drive).
- **Need:** A unified "Central Intelligence" view to assign tasks, oversee agent activity, and monitor business health (MRR, Token burn).

### Primary Agent: The Autonomous Employee
- **Role:** Task-specific specialist (Analysis, PRD, SOW, Onboarding).
- **Goal:** Execute end-to-end business processes with 92%+ accuracy and minimal human input.
- **Need:** Context-awareness via `llms.txt`, clear rules via `agent.md`, and deterministic triggers from the dashboard/Supabase.
- **Interactions:** Must be aware of and interact with other "OpenClaw" agents in the swarm.

### Secondary Users
- **Future White-label Clients:** SME owners who will receive a forked version of this system (Roadmap item).
