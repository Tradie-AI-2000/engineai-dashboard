## Synthesis & Reflection

### Reflection Checkpoint
The dashboard must act as a "Unified Integration Hub" connecting a vast array of existing communication and productivity tools within a 7-day sprint. It must strictly adhere to the premium, minimalist Engine AI brand identity (Dark Mode, Gold accents, "No fluff").

---

## Final Constraints

### Technical Parameters
- **Integrations:**
    - Telegram (Inbound/Outbound bot comms)
    - Discord (Agent operations channel)
    - Google Drive (File indexing and retrieval)
    - engineai.co.nz & Discovery Web App (Lead ingestion)
    - Google Workspace (Calendar/Meet/Gmail access)
    - Claude Ecosystem (Open agents + Individual co-work setups)
- **Architecture:** Must leverage Vercel AI SDK v6 and MCP for these bridges.

### Timeline Parameters
- **Target:** 1 Week for a functional MVP.
- **Priority:** Rapid deployment of the "Lead-to-Build" autonomous loop.

### Brand & Aesthetic Parameters
- **Aesthetic:** Dark, premium, gold-accented.
- **Vibe:** "Precise, confident, and minimal. No corporate fluff. No generic SaaS aesthetic."
- **Core Colors:** `#0A0A0A` (Background), `#C4A35A` (Gold).
- **Tone:** Direct, grounded, operator-focused.

### Flexibility
- **Scope:** The user is committed to the full vision; the primary flexibility is in the **refinement phase** after the initial "build-everything" sprint.
